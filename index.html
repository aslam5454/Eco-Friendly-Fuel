About Us:

Welcome to Our Save Enviromentental Website, a collaborative effort by B.Tech CSE Engineering students from Lovely Professional University. Our CA-2 project in the EVS subject explores the vital theme of "Environmental Impacts of Tourism" through the lenses of technology and environmental science. In this concise space, we present insightful analyses and tech-driven solutions—from carbon footprint assessments to smart waste management strategies. Designed for students, environmental enthusiasts, and industry players, Save Enviromentental Website offers a succinct yet impactful exploration of how technology can mitigate the environmental footprint of tourism. Join us on this journey towards a more sustainable future where travel enhances our lives without compromising the health of our planet. Thank you for being part of the conversation.
